Synopsis

Upon being awarded the Pulitzer at columbia University, in New York, journalist Cleiton Harris receives an unusual invitation from the Seattle Post to investigate the disappearance of an American in Brazil.


As his investigations progress, he comes across an international network specializing in kidnapping and organ trafficking. But when he meets his Brazilian friend Edwardo, an engineer, ex-combatant of the Foreign Legion, a chain of events leads him to discover that he was not chosen by chance.


A diabolical plan is under way and his friend is part of the puzzle. Persecutions, secrets from the past, brutal murders and an atmosphere of tension will make Cleiton understand that in the criminal underworld, justice is dark.
